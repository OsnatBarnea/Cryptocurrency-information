About the website

The site displays the "strongest" 100 virtual coins (cryptocoins).
The data is taken from the site CoinGecko
Information for the graphs was taken from CryptoCompare
In this site you can get information about a specific coin (name, nick-name, currency and symbol).
By clicking the "more information" button in the "coins" page you can see the current value.
The button displays the current value in dollars, euros and shekels of the specific coin.

You can also search for specific coins by using the search box.
To display the change of values of specific coins, you can press the "Reports" link after choosing the coins of interest.

I hope you find this site interesting and useful.
